About CSAW / 01

Two decades of cyber excellence

CSAW (see-SAW) was founded in 2003 by students of Professor Nasir Memon, co-founder of NYU Tandon's cybersecurity program. What began as a local competition has grown into the world's most comprehensive student-run cybersecurity event.

Directed by Ramesh Karri, CSAW continuously evolves to meet the changing threat landscape — from additive manufacturing to machine learning, cloud-based AI, and beyond.

Participants range from high school students to doctoral candidates, united by a passion for cybersecurity and a desire to push boundaries.

CSAW'25 Timeline

Mark your calendar. Every competition follows a qualifier → finals pipeline.

Jul 2025
Phase 1 · Open
Registration Opens
All competition registrations open. Teams form, advisors register, and regional qualifiers begin accepting entries.
Sep 2025
Phase 2 · Qualifying
Global Online Qualifiers
48-hour CTF qualifier runs online. All other competitions launch their online qualification rounds simultaneously across all regions.
Oct 2025
Phase 3 · Selection
Finalist Announcements
Top teams per region invited to regional finals. Travel grants available for qualifying student teams.
Nov 5–7
Phase 4 · Finals
CSAW'25 Finals & Conference
Three days of in-person competitions, keynotes, career fair, workshops, and awards ceremony in Brooklyn, New York.
